*Cache Valley AI Community is a brand-new group. we started forming in April 2026 and are just getting people together.*

## Who We Are
A local community in Cache Valley, UT for anyone curious about or working with artificial intelligence and machine learning. Whether you build with AI every day, are just starting to explore, or simply want to understand where things are heading — you're welcome.

## What We Do
We meet to **learn** and **connect** sharing what we're working on, what we're curious about, and what we're figuring out as the AI landscape keeps shifting.

## When We Meet
We're aiming for **bi-monthly meetups**. No fixed schedule yet, we're still finding our rhythm.

## How to Join
The fastest way in is the Discord — say hi, lurk, or jump into a thread. Prefer quieter nudges? The email list is a second path in.
