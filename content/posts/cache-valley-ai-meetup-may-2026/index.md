### Event Details: Cache Valley AI Community
- **Date**: Friday, May 8th, 2026
- **Time**: 12:00 PM (MST)
- **Location**: Beehive Grill, Logan, UT

## Event Overview
Round two of the Cache Valley AI Meetup, same place and same energy. Come over lunch to connect, share what you're working on, and talk through what's actually useful in AI right now. No slides, no presentations, just lunch and good conversation.

If you missed the April meetup, this is the perfect excuse to show up. We'll catch you up on what people have been up to, what they've been seeing in the wild, and where things might be headed next.

## Why Attend This Meetup?
Show up if you're:
- **Developers and engineers** working with (or curious about) AI and machine learning
- **Students and educators** interested in the practical side of ML
- **Tech enthusiasts** who want to connect with others in Cache Valley's AI community
- **Anyone** interested in where AI is going and how to be part of it
